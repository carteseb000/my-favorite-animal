About Bearded Dragons.

what bearded dragons eat.

Bearded dragons can eat crickets, wax-worms, meal-worms, and most greens. Also they can eat Dandelions.


Where Bearded Dragons Live.

Bearded dragons are native in Australia. Bearded dragons can live in very hot and very dry places.


What colors Bearded Dragons come in.

Bearded dragons come in Red, Orange, Yellow, Tangerine, Sand-fire Red, Citrus Tiger
